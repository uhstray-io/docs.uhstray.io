**Code**
```
1: a system of principles or rules
2: instructions for a computer (as within a piece of software)
```

**Network**
```
1: to distribute for broadcast on a  network
2: to join (things, such as communities, events, or projects) in a network
3: a usually informally interconnected group or association of persons (such as friends or professional colleagues)
```

**Infrastructure** 
```
1: the underlying foundation or basic framework (as of a system or organization)
2: the manner in which the components of a computer or computer system are organized and integrated
```

**Service**
```
1: to repair or provide maintenance for
2: to perform any of the business functions auxiliary to production or distribution of
```
